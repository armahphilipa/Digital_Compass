Flexible Schedules

Choose a schedule that suits your life. Our 12-month full-time and 18-month part-time options let you learn at your own pace without disrupting your other commitments.

Income-Based Repayment

With Digital Compass, you pay only when you succeed. No upfront costs—just a 10% income contribution for five years once you’re employed, so you can focus on learning without financial stress.

Empower Individuals

Our community is here to guide you. Connect with mentors, alumni, and peers who offer support during and after the program. Graduates can even join as mentors or facilitators, paying it forward.
